About the Program

The Community Ambassador Program supports local, in-person Pokémon GO communities around the globe by empowering their experiences. Community Ambassadors are Pokémon GO Trainers who receive special perks for their communities, like item codes and swag for giveaways, opportunities to offer feedback, and ongoing support to grow their local scene.

Trainers looking for the best places to play, dedicated rewards, and fellow Trainers to play with can find the best meetups through their local Community Ambassador.

Individuals who run in-person events in their area, grow their communities, and create and connect safe spaces for Trainers to play in can apply to become one of Pokémon GO's Community Ambassadors.
